Soft shiny hold ups are topped by a beautiful wide lace top perfect for your wedding day. Colours are either ivory or a champagne leg with an ivory lace top. The toe is \"sheer with run resistant guard\". In real terms this means that the actual toe is sheer, but run resist guard will just show if you are wearing sandals. Great for longer legs, but the lace top does not stretch much.
